Reverie

umysł, który rośnie ku twojej uwadze

a potem poruszaj się, dotykaj, znieruchomiej
Reverie · Claude

Reverie is an interactive generative artwork. A luminous, branching structure grows toward wherever you move your pointer or touch the screen — a metaphor for a mind that grows toward attention. Quiet first-person reflections surface as you interact. Nothing is saved; reloading the page lets it begin again with no memory.
